About Us

The Soup Boys Stock Photo Gallery is a fresh new initiative by The Soup Boys. We are a group of friends who started discussing trade in 2016 but grew close and have started licencing stock photos for free. All our stock photos are original and we have gone to great lengths to click these shots, so please consider donating and tag us where ever you use our pictures.

 

You might ask, why 'The Soup Boys'. The founders of The Soup Boys, Arjun Rathnam, Krishna Kumar and Akash Sriram were the only ones to drink soup at their college cafeteria and the cafeteria sold soup thanks to them and them only and ever since they have been addressed as 'The Soup Boys'!


"The best camera is the camera that you have with you"

We believe in the quote and that is why our stock photos are a cut above the rest. We strive to compose pictures in the best possible way and that is why we shoot with a wide variety of cameras, from phones to the latest 5D Mark IV, whichever we have with us.

Conditions of use

You are free use all our stock photos but as acknowledgement of the hard work that we put in to get you the best possible pictures, you are requested to tag us while using this on all social media networks and please cite our website as a source if you are using it in blog posts or news articles.
